Project Overview


Problem Statement

Group trip planning can feel like a lonely and maddening internal dialog: where to go, who can afford it, expectations, vibes, and timing. Organizers face stress and grueling research across platforms. A simpler way to consolidate vacation planning is needed. Wouldn't it be nice to structure this babble with a supportive AI?

Key Features

  • Chat-Based Interaction: Users can plan their trips by simply chatting with the app, making the process intuitive and user-friendly.
  • AI-Powered Recommendations: The app provides personalized suggestions for destinations, hotels, and activities based on user preferences and group dynamics.
  • Collaborative Planning: Group members can contribute to the planning process, ensuring everyone's preferences are considered.

My Role

As the lead UX designer for Travel Babble, I was responsible for conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and performing usability testing to refine the user experience. My goal was to ensure that the app was not only functional but also engaging and easy to use for all group members involved in trip planning.
