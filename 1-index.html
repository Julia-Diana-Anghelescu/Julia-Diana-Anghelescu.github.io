HI! I'M JULIA, UX/UI DESIGNER

I am a UX designer who combines creativity, aesthetic, problem-solving skills and empathy! I love to create user experiences that leave a lasting impact, infusing the digital world with a sense of style and sophistication.

Communication

I value honesty above all. Transparency builds trust and keeps projects moving forward smoothly. I'd rather admit a mistake and learn from it than hide it.

Passion

I bring energy and care to every project. My passion fuels creativity, motivates teams, and ensures every detail contributes to a meaningful user experience.

Reliability

If I commit to something, I deliver. You can rely on me to meet deadlines, maintain quality, and take full ownership of my work.

Design that connects

I create seamless, user-focused experiences that balance beauty and functionality. Every design decision is intentional, aiming to make interactions simple, intuitive, and impactful.

Collaborative by nature

I thrive in cross-functional teams, blending ideas from design, development, and strategy. Collaboration helps transform complex challenges into clear, user-centered solutions.

Continuous growth

I'm always learning and refining my craft. Every project is an opportunity to evolve; improving how I design, communicate, and create value for users and teams.
